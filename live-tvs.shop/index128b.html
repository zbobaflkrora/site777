Payment Methods

We accept PayPal, Visa/MasterCard via PayPal Gateway.

How to pay with Visa/Mastercard ?

You need to pay with visa/mastercard via PayPal Gateway:

– Select PayPal payment method

– At the next page select second option as “Create an account” or “Pay with Credit or Debit Card”

– Next page enter your card information and billing address and pay.

Can I get a Refund ?

~ According to “terms of the service” purchased subscription are not refundable because it causes cost to provide.

~ If you have purchased an order and we haven’t delivered it to you yet, you can ask to cancel the order and we send you a full refund.

~ Before purchasing a service and pay, you can contact us via email Contact@theextreamvision.com

How can I subscribe for the service ?

~ Go to pricing in home page to see plans and prices. 

~ Select your favourite service plan and you can pay via PayPal, Visa, Mastercard.

~ We will deliver your order between 5 mins to 12 Hours manually.

Can I cancel the Order ?

According to “terms of the service” purchased subscription are not refundable because it causes cost to provide.

If you have purchased an order and we haven’t delivered it to you yet, you can ask to cancel the order and we send you a full refund.

How long does it take for orders to proceed ?

We usually process orders manually (because of security purposes).
So please be Patient after payment. We will send your order details to your provided email address between 5 minutes to 12 hours.
Please contact us if you haven’t received your order after 12 hours and check your inbox/spam/bulk folder first.
